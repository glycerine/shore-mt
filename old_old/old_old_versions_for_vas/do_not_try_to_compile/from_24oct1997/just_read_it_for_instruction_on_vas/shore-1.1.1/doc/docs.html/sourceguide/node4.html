Documentation and Examples (docs.ps, docs.html, and examples)

These three directories are at the root of a documentation release.

docs.ps
document and manual pages as Postscript documents suitable for printing.
docs.ps
document and manual pages as html (hypertext markup language) pages suitable for viewing with a web browser such as Mosaic or Netscape.

examples
a variety of example programs, described more fully below. Users at your installation who wish to learn more about Shore may wish to copy one or more of the sub-directories of SHROOT/installed/examples to their own workspace to compile them. The examples are designed to be usable in conjunction with a binary release (a source release is not required).
